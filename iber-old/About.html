About Us

Iberrtech is a Branding and Advertising agency. In fact, we are the most cost effective Digital Marketing Company covering the entire state of Kerala. Our services include Branding, Web Design, Graphic Design, Bulk SMS, E-Mail Marketing, Social Media Marketing, App Development, Offline Marketing, PPC Campaign and SEO. We are located in Malappuram (established in Perinthalmanna in 2022) as the sister concern of Digital Space ( established in Calicut in 2017). We have been providing guaranteed, scalable, result-oriented services for a variety of businesses, from small start-ups to established, leading firms. Our professional and dedicated team always renders you our immense experience and skill to build your brand brick by brick.
